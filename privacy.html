Section 03

Social Media & Portfolio Use

I love sharing the work we create together! However, your privacy and comfort are always the top priority.

During our communication or booking process, I will ask for your explicit consent regarding whether or not you are comfortable having your images shared on my personal social media accounts (such as Instagram) or featured here in my web portfolio. You are under no obligation to say yes.

Please note that all images currently displayed on this website feature subjects who have previously granted me permission, and these images have already been posted appropriately on my public pages. I will never publish your photos without your clear approval.

Section 04

Payment & Booking

All bookings are confirmed only upon receipt of a deposit or full payment as agreed upon in writing. Submitting a contact form does not constitute a confirmed booking.

  • A deposit will be required to secure your date, the amount of which will be communicated in the process of booking the shoot.
  • Remaining balances are due on or before the date of the shoot unless otherwise agreed.
  • Accepted payment methods will be confirmed while booking the shoot.
  • All prices are subject to change; quoted prices are valid for 30 days from date of quote.
Section 05

Cancellations & Rescheduling

Life happens, and I am always willing to work something out. That said, the following general guidelines will apply:

  • Cancellations with more than 7 days notice — deposits may be applied to a rescheduled date. If it is an outright cancellation, the depsoit will be returned.
  • Cancellations with less than 24 hours notice — deposits are non-refundable.
  • Rescheduling — subject to availability; please give as much notice as possible. Please feel free to reach out in situations like this in the interest of both parties.
  • Cancellation by photographer — a full refund of any deposits paid will be issued.

All cancellation requests must be made in writing via email or the contact form on this site.

Section 06

Deliverables & Turnaround

Delivery timelines and the number of edited images will be agreed upon at the time of booking. As a general guide:

  • Edited images are delivered digitally via an online gallery or file transfer link. This will typically be through a service like Google Drive or any other method agreed upon at the time of booking.
  • Standard turnaround is typically 1–2 weeks from the date of the shoot unless specified otherwise.
  • Rush delivery may be available for an additional fee — discuss at time of booking
  • RAW files are not included unless explicitly agreed upon. My job as a photographer is to provide clients with the best quality images possible, which involves the editing process of RAW files.

The photographer retains the right to select which images are delivered. Not all images taken during a session will be included in the final gallery. If a specific image is requested that was not included in the delivered gallery, I will review the request and determine if it can be provided.

Privacy Policy

It is never sold or shared with third parties for marketing purposes. The information is sent to me via email and stored securely on my personal devices.

Data Collection

The following types of personal data may be collected when you use this site:

You may request deletion of any personal data held by contacting me directly. Requests will be fulfilled within a reasonable timeframe.
