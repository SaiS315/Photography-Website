About the eyes
behind the camera.

My name is Saikrishna Sunkara, a 21 year old college student and freelance photographer based in the Bay Area, CA and Madison, WI. Photography is a hobby I picked up about two years ago, and quickly turned into a passion of mine that has taken me incredible places and helped me meet and connect with many amazing people along the way. For the moment, I don't specialize in any one type of photography, as I'm still learning and exploring everything the field has to offer. So whether you need a portraits for a new grad, photos on the race track, a beautiful landscape for your phone or wall at home, or photos of a grand event, there's a good chance I've probably done it. If not, I'm always excited to learn something new along the way and take on a challenge!

My Instagram page (@eyes_of_sai) linked below is where I post a lot of my work, and if you want to reach out to me to chat about a potential photoshoot or just chat about photography, you can shoot me a message on there. You can also use my website here to reach out to me as well, where I will check requests and reach out to you as soon as I can. I look forward to working with you and creating some magic together!
